My Story

From pixel-perfect design to complete marketing operations.

I started my career with a passion for visual design and a belief that aesthetics alone aren't enough — every creative decision should drive a business outcome. Over 10+ years, I evolved from a graphic designer into a full-service marketing specialist who understands the entire pipeline: from brand strategy to social media execution, from product photography to e-commerce management.

My education in Marketing & Media Communications at the Faculty of Culture and Media gave me the strategic foundation. But the real education happened in the field — working with 15+ brands across baby retail, luxury watches, hospitality, automotive, food service, and home decor.

Today, I operate as a one-person creative department. I don't just design — I think about why we're designing, who we're reaching, and what happens after the click. That combination of strategic thinking and hands-on production is what makes my work different.

Marketing Philosophy

Sell the feeling, not the product. Every campaign I build starts with the customer's emotional reality — what they need to feel, believe, or imagine before they buy. Then I reverse-engineer the visuals, copy, and delivery to create that moment.

I believe in consistent brand presence, developmental value positioning for premium products, and campaigns that respect the customer's intelligence while creating urgency through aspiration, not pressure.
